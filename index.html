Our Story

Sooke's kitchen table since 1963.

Mom's Cafe opened in 1963 and has been serving the same honest, homestyle food ever since. We've fed three generations of Sooke families, every Juan de Fuca trail hiker who knew to stop here, and a few people who got a little lost and ended up staying for pie.

Nothing fancy. No gimmicks. Good food, generous portions, fair prices, and a room full of familiar faces. That's always been enough.
